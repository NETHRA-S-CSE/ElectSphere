Your secure and easy digital voting platform

Cast Your Vote in 4 Easy Steps

Step 1: Login to your account
Enter your Voter ID number to begin the voting process. This helps us verify your identity and ensure you haven’t already voted.
Step 2: Upload Your Voter ID Image
Upload a clear image of your official Voter ID card. This will be used to validate your identity (and for face matching).
Step 3: Capture Your Live Face
Use your device’s webcam to take a live photo. (If face authentication is enabled, this step confirms you are the rightful voter.)
Step 4: Cast Your Vote
Once verified, you’ll be shown the list of candidates. Simply select your preferred candidate and click “Submit Vote.”
