Welcome to ElectSphere...!

Your secure and easy digital voting platform

Cast Your Vote in 4 Easy Steps

Step 1: Login to your account
Log in using your registered mobile number and password to begin the voting process.
Step 2: View Family Members
If your family is registered under the same account, you can view their voting status and manage their access.
Step 3: Verify OTP
You’ll receive a One-Time Password (OTP) on your mobile. Enter it to confirm your identity.
Step 4: Cast Your Vote
Once verified, you’ll be shown the list of candidates. Simply select your preferred candidate and click “Submit Vote.”
